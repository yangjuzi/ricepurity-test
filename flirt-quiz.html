About the Flirt Quiz

The Flirt Quiz is a fun assessment that evaluates your flirting skills and natural charm. This test explores how you interact with people you're attracted to and your ability to create romantic connections through conversation and body language.

How it works: You'll answer 16 questions about your behavior in romantic situations, your confidence level, and how you approach people you're interested in. Each question explores different aspects of flirting including charm, confidence, playfulness, and social skills.

Scoring: Your final result will categorize you as either "Shy & Sweet," "Naturally Charming," "Charming Flirt," "Master Flirt," or "Irresistible Charmer" based on your responses. The test analyzes your answers across multiple dimensions of flirting ability.

Remember: Flirting is about making genuine connections and having fun! The best flirting comes from being authentic, confident, and respectful. Whether you're naturally charming or more reserved, everyone has their own unique appeal!

Note: Good flirting is always respectful and consensual. The goal is to make both people feel good and create positive connections.
