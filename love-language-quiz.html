About the Love Language Quiz

The Love Language Quiz is based on Dr. Gary Chapman's groundbreaking research on how people express and receive love. This assessment helps you understand your primary love language and how you can better connect with your partner.

How it works: You'll answer 15 questions about your preferences in relationships and how you like to give and receive affection. Each question explores different ways of expressing love including words, actions, time, touch, and gifts.

The 5 Love Languages: Words of Affirmation (verbal appreciation), Quality Time (focused attention), Physical Touch (appropriate physical connection), Acts of Service (helpful actions), and Receiving Gifts (thoughtful presents).

Remember: Understanding your love language can dramatically improve your relationships! When you know how you and your partner prefer to give and receive love, you can communicate more effectively and feel more connected.

Tip: Most people have a primary love language, but you might also resonate with secondary languages. The key is understanding what makes you feel most loved and appreciated.
