Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Rice Purity Test, your privacy is our top priority. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our website and services.

Information We DON'T Collect

We are proud to say that we do not collect, store, or track the following:

  • Your test responses or answers
  • Your test scores or results
  • Personal identifying information
  • Email addresses or contact information (unless voluntarily provided)
  • Browsing history or behavior tracking
  • Location data

How the Test Works

The Rice Purity Test is processed entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your responses never leave your device or get transmitted to our servers. All calculations are performed locally, ensuring complete privacy and anonymity.

Cookies and Local Storage

We may use local storage to temporarily save your test progress, allowing you to resume if you accidentally close the browser. This data is stored only on your device and is automatically deleted when you complete the test or clear your browser data.

Third-Party Services

Our website may use third-party services for analytics and advertising. These services may collect anonymous usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies. We do not share any personal information with these services.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@ricepurity-test.app
