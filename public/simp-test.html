About the Simp Test

The Simp Test is a personality assessment that evaluates whether you might be overly generous, accommodating, or self-sacrificing in romantic relationships. The term "simp" refers to someone who goes to excessive lengths to gain attention or affection from someone they're attracted to.

How it works: You'll answer 20 questions about your behavior in relationships, dating scenarios, and how you treat people you're attracted to. Each question presents two options - one representing balanced behavior and another representing potentially excessive generosity or attention-seeking validation.

Scoring: Your final result will categorize you as either "Confident & Balanced," "Slightly Generous," "People Pleaser," "Overly Generous," or "Ultimate Simp" based on your responses. The test analyzes your answers across multiple dimensions including self-respect, boundaries, generosity, and validation-seeking behavior.

Remember: This test is designed for self-reflection and entertainment. Being generous and caring is wonderful, but healthy relationships require balance and mutual respect. There's no shame in any result - use it as an opportunity for self-awareness!
