About the Am I Pansexual Quiz

This quiz is designed as a safe space for self-reflection about pansexual identity and attractions. It's important to understand that sexual orientation is deeply personal and cannot be determined by any quiz.

How it works: You'll answer 17 thoughtful questions about your feelings, attractions, and experiences with people of all genders and gender identities. These questions are designed to help you reflect on your own emotions in a supportive environment.

Important to know: Pansexuality is attraction to people regardless of their gender identity. It's about being attracted to the person rather than their gender. This can include attraction to cisgender, transgender, non-binary, and gender-fluid individuals.

Remember: Your identity is valid regardless of the results. This quiz is meant to support your journey of self-discovery, not define you. Take your time, be honest with yourself, and know that whatever you discover about yourself is perfectly okay.

Support Resources: If you're questioning your sexual orientation, consider reaching out to LGBTQ+ support organizations, counselors, or trusted friends and family members who can provide support during your journey.
