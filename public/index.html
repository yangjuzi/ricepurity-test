Everything you need to know about our colorful Rice Purity Test

The Rice Purity Test is a fun self-graded survey consisting of 100 questions about life experiences. Originally created at Rice University, it's designed to measure a person's supposed degree of innocence in various life areas. Scores range from 0 to 100, with higher scores indicating fewer life experiences. Our version makes it more colorful and engaging! üåà
