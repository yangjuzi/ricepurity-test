About This Exploration Quiz

This quiz is designed as a safe space for self-reflection about your feelings, attractions, and identity. It's important to understand that sexual orientation is deeply personal and cannot be determined by any quiz.

How it works: You'll answer 15 thoughtful questions about your feelings, attractions, and experiences. These questions are designed to help you reflect on your own emotions and attractions in a supportive environment.

Important to know: Only you can determine your sexual orientation. This quiz is simply a tool for self-reflection and exploration. Sexual orientation exists on a spectrum, and everyone's journey of self-discovery is unique and valid.

Remember: Your identity is valid regardless of the results. This quiz is meant to support your journey of self-discovery, not define you. Take your time, be honest with yourself, and know that whatever you discover about yourself is perfectly okay.

Support Resources: If you're questioning your sexual orientation, consider reaching out to LGBTQ+ support organizations, counselors, or trusted friends and family members who can provide support during your journey.
