About Us

Learn more about our mission and the team behind Rice Purity Test

Our Mission

At Rice Purity Test, we believe in creating a fun, safe, and anonymous environment for people to explore and reflect on their life experiences. Our mission is to provide the most comprehensive and accurate Rice Purity Test available online, while maintaining the highest standards of privacy and user experience.

We've reimagined the classic Rice University purity test for the modern digital age, enhancing it with detailed analytics, beautiful design, and meaningful insights that help users better understand themselves.

Our platform is completely free, requires no registration, and ensures complete anonymity. We believe self-discovery should be accessible to everyone, without barriers or judgment.

Our Story

The Rice Purity Test originated at Rice University as a way for students to bond and share experiences in a lighthearted manner. What started as a simple campus tradition has evolved into a cultural phenomenon, taken by millions of people worldwide.

We recognized the need for a modern, well-designed version of this beloved test that maintains its spirit while providing a better user experience. Our team consists of passionate developers, designers, and psychologists who worked together to create the most engaging Rice Purity Test experience available.

Since launching, we've helped over 2 million people discover their purity scores and gain insights into their life experiences. We're proud to continue this tradition of fun, anonymous self-reflection.
